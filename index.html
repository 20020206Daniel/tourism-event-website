Welcome to the Festival

Festival Location

The Maletsunyane Cultural and Adventure Festival is one of the most exciting tourism events in Lesotho.The event takes place near the famous Maletsunyane Falls in Semonkong and attracts both local and international tourists

Visitors experience Basotho culture, traditional music, adventure sports,local food,and breathtaking landscapes.The festival celebrates heritage while promoting tourism development in Lesotho

Event Details

  • Date: 10 - 12 December 2026
  • Location: Semonkong, Lesotho
  • Activities: Music, culture, adventure sports, food festival
